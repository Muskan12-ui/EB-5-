Free End-to-End EB-5 Consultancy

The Calculus provides complete, professional EB-5 advisory services — from initial eligibility assessment through Green Card approval and beyond — entirely free of additional consultancy charges. Our fees are structured through the EB-5 process itself, so you never receive a separate consultancy bill.

Eligibility Assessment

A thorough review of your personal and financial profile against current EB-5 requirements. We assess your investment readiness, source-of-funds documentation needs, and timeline expectations to create a realistic, honest roadmap.

Project Selection

Evaluate qualifying EB-5 projects through our tier-1 U.S. regional-center partner. We help you understand project structures, risk factors, job-creation models, and independently audited track records before you commit.

Documentation Assistance

Help organising and preparing all required paperwork correctly — the single most important factor in a smooth EB-5 case. We guide you through every document required for your I-526E petition.

Source of Funds Guidance

Navigate India's regulatory requirements — RBI liberalised remittance scheme, FEMA compliance, tax documentation — for lawful and verifiable source-of-funds documentation that USCIS requires.

Attorney Coordination

Liaise with experienced U.S. immigration attorneys on your behalf. We coordinate the legal preparation, review petition documents, and ensure clear communication between you and your legal team.

Banking Assistance

Guidance on U.S. bank account setup, financial onboarding, and navigating the transition between Indian and American banking systems.

Foreign Remittance Support

End-to-end assistance with RBI/FEMA-compliant international fund transfers. We help ensure your investment capital reaches the U.S. through proper channels with complete documentation.

USCIS Process Updates

Regular status updates and timeline tracking throughout your case. We keep you informed at every milestone — from petition receipt to approval, interview, and Green Card issuance.

Relocation Planning

City selection, housing search, schooling research, neighbourhood orientation, and comprehensive soft-landing support for your family's transition to the United States.

Continued Family Support

Our relationship does not end when your Green Card arrives. We continue to support your family with ongoing guidance, professional referrals, and advisory for years to come.

Our professional consultancy is provided completely FREE of charge throughout your entire EB-5 journey.
