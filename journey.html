The EB-5 journey

Many families worry that EB-5 is complicated. It doesn't have to be. We break the process into eight clear steps and stay beside you at every one — from your first consultation to your family's permanent residency, and beyond.

Eight steps
01

Consultation & eligibility review

We understand your goals and profile, and explain the current picture honestly.

02

Project selection

Evaluate a suitable, qualifying EB-5 project with our tier-1 U.S. regional-center partner.

03

Source-of-funds documentation

Prepare and organise your paperwork correctly — the single biggest factor in a smooth case.

04

Investment & I-526E filing

You invest and file your EB-5 petition with USCIS.

05

Conditional Green Card

Granted to the investor and eligible family members.

06

Relocation support

We help your family settle into your chosen U.S. city.

07

Business acquisition (optional)

Identify and acquire a running U.S. business or franchise.

08

Permanent residency & citizenship

Remove conditions (I-829) and look ahead to citizenship.

A realistic timeline from India
StageApproximate time
Select project + prepare documents (incl. transfer of $800,000)1–3 months
File I-526E — Rural set-aside (priority)~1–2 years
File I-526E — High-Unemployment Area~2–3 years
File I-526E — Unreserved~3–5 years
Consular processing in India (NVC, medical, interview)~6–12+ months

EB-5 is not an instant Green Card. Plan around 2–5 years to the conditional Green Card. Timelines are estimates, never guarantees.

Source of funds, simply

U.S. authorities must see that your invested capital was earned and transferred lawfully — salary and savings, the sale of property or shares, business income, a gift, or an inheritance, each supported by clean documentation. Getting this right is where experienced advisory matters most, and it is where The Calculus does the heavy lifting with you.
